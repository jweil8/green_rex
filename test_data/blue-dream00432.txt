"In a lot of ways, Blue Dream epitomizes where cannabis is heading.  It's a crowd pleaser.  It works.  It's reliable.

The truth is, that makes Blue Dream a little 'boring'.  Staid almost (like anything cannabis can be 'staid').  It definitely isn't sexy.

If we were comparing it to red wine, we'd call it a 'quaffing wine'.  Great for a party, maybe not the top choice for a gourmet dinner.

But if you're having a burg..."
